About us

Pure Source Recruitment commenced in Brisbane in February 2004, and was created from a desire to provide a personalised recruitment service to both employers and job seekers.

It has since grown into one of Brisbane’s leading specialist recruitment agencies, and continues to set the standard for Client and Candidate care.

Pure Source Recruitment specialises in filling positions across a range of industries that include;

  • Building and Construction
  • Engineering
  • Mining and resources
  • Oil and Gas
  • Property
  • Health and Education
  • I.T.
  • Finance and Insurance
  • Professional Services
  • Charity and not for profit organisations

Services Include;

  • End to End Recruitment of Permanent Staff
  • Temporary and Contract staff
  • Tailored Recruitment Services based on the specific needs of your business
  • Psychometric Testing and Skills Testing
  • Human Resources Consultancy

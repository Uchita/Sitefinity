About Us

Us

People Consulting Professional was born out of the desire to treat our business partner's with a higher level of professional service. 

With over 30 years combined recruitment experience, People Consulting partner with an exclusive number of organisations. This enables us to gain a greater depth of understanding of their needs and objectives.

Based in Sydney, we have a national and international network of contacts to source challenging roles and unique talent.  

Our thinking is not constrained by borders and time zones.  We believe in the global mobility of the workforce and the opportunities it will create. 


Our Methodology

Our methodology has been built up from working in the recruitment industry for many years, locally and overseas.  The one thing we have learned, there is no magic bullet.

Our approach is professional and ethical.  As all recruitment should be.

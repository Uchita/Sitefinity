About Us

SilverSky is a specialist recruitment company dedicated to making a difference in the recruitment process. We work closely with our Candidates to unearth career solutions and we partner with our clients to undertake strategic recruitment initiatives throughout Australia meeting their needs in a timely and efficient manner. We pride ourselves on the relationships we have forged over the years and know that this is only possible by us continuing to deliver results.

By taking the time to understand the ambitions and capabilities of our Candidates and the companies that we partner with, rather than just skills and position descriptions, SilverSky places people into careers, not just jobs. In the long term, this reduces stress, costs and frustration for everyone we work with.

Our combined experience recruiting in Australia and Internationally encompasses multiple disciplines enabling us to share innovative solutions based on experience.

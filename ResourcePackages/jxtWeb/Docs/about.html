Who is JXT?

JXT is a leading cloud-based digital marketing solutions provider for recruiters and corporate hiring teams. The race for talent acquisition starts with creating a great digital marketing ecosystem. Every day, we work with a growing base of over 800 recruiters globally such as Adecco, Kelly, Manpower to combine the best in class functions on a single, robust platform to help them create the best candidate and employer experience.

Our solutions and professional services enable your business to bring together the multiple components of your career site, social media, email marketing, job board, CRM and search engine tactics to one responsive site. By enabling your online interaction with your audience to be seamless in real time, you optimise your online brand and enhance your customer experience. We understand the recruitment industry and have the depth of experience in implementing the best solutions for you to achieve positive returns on your business investments.

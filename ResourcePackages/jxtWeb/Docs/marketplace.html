Bullhorn Recruitment CRM

Bullhorn is the worlds largest and most recognised recruitment CRM software provider. A truly global business, Bullhorn has over 500 employees and offces in Sydney, Melbourne, Singapore, London, Amsterdam, and Boston. Its data capture and customer insight technology puts the most up-to- date and powerful information at users fingertips to give them everything they need to win clients and keep them happy. Today, Bullhorn serves more than 10,000 clients and 350,000 users globally. Its mobile, customisable, and intuitive software solutions are used by some of the worlds most prominent recruitment agencies.

Product Overview

Bullhorn provides the fastest and most feature-rich recruitment CRM and applicant tracking technology in the world. Many applicant tracking systems are just glorified databases, reducing complex relationships into simple records. Bullhorn's patented CRM functionality is completely automated allowing your consultants to focus on strengthening client relationships while Bullhorn does the rest.

integrated services

With implementation packages tailored to your specific needs, Bullhorn and JXT form the ideal marketing and recruitment data capture solution. Build your marketing platform with JXT and watch as captured data integrates automatically into the Bullhorn Recruitment CRM. Bullhorn also has the largest 3rd party 'Marketplace' of any recruitment CRM provider in the world, allowing other best-of-breed recruitment software vendors to plug into the system seamlessly.

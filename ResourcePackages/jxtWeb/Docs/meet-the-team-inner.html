meet the team

Rick Mare

CEO and Founder

Dynamic, strategic digital lead with an eye for forefront thinking. Rick is the founder of JXT, a global digital marketing solutions provider in recruitment. Having grown to over 800 success stories over 23 countries and 17 languages, he ardently campaigns for the best user experience for recruiters and candidates.

Rick is an active advocate for enabling the future of recruitment user interaction to be intuitive and positive. His wish is to combine his knowledge and experience to build the best recruitment digital marketing ecosystem for his clients and their audiences. Rick is enthusiastic about golf, sailing and wildlife protection causes.
